About the Shop

I run Homegrown Spirits with my partner — we do sand-etched glasswork, custom pieces, that kind of thing. I also make wood items, stickers, prints, and some digital 3D models.

Payments are processed securely through PayPal — you can pay with any credit/debit card, PayPal balance, or Venmo. I never see your payment details.

Shipping & Policies

Shipping

Handmade items ship within 3–5 business days via USPS. Print-on-demand items ship directly from the printer (5–10 days).

Returns

If something arrives damaged, email me within 14 days with a photo and I'll make it right — replacement or full refund.

Payments

All payments processed through PayPal. I never see or store your card information.

Digital Items

Digital files are delivered via email after payment. No returns on digital downloads.
